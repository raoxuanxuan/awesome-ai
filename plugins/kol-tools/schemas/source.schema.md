# Source Page Schema

Use for `wiki/sources/*.md`.

## Purpose

A source page summarizes evidence for one durable topic. It is not a place for
free-form persona writing.

## Required Sections

```text
# <Topic>

## Scope
## Key Evidence
## Recurring Claims
## Contradictions Or Open Questions
## Related Methods
## Related Positions
## Evidence
```

## Evidence Rules

- Every durable claim must cite at least one tweet id.
- Keep tweet id, date, URL when available, and a short quote or paraphrase.
- Mark reply evidence as reply evidence when relevant.
- Do not quote subscriber/private evidence into public-facing text.

## Update Rules

- Append small incremental updates under a dated or pack-id section.
- Merge repeated claims only after review.
- Do not update `soul.md` from a source update unless the delta changes a
  stable persona-level pattern.
